# We Move NY — Changelog

---

## 🚀 Latest Release — April 2026

### ✨ New Features

- **Profile Photos** — Tap your avatar to upload a photo. It's automatically cropped and resized so it looks great everywhere.
- **Swap Templates** — Save any swap as a template and load it instantly next time you post. Huge time-saver for regular posters.
- **Mutual Matches** — Post your swap and we automatically surface operators whose schedules fit yours. No more scrolling through dozens of posts.
- **Language Support** — Switch between English, Español, and 中文 from your profile. More languages coming.
- **Read Receipts** — See ✓ when your message is delivered and ✓✓ in blue when it's been read.
- **Block User** — Block anyone from messaging you from inside the conversation.
- **Verified Operator Badge** — A blue shield appears next to the name of operators verified by your union steward.
- **Help & FAQ** — Tap "Help" on your profile for answers to the most common questions about how swaps work.
- **Feedback Button** — See something wrong? Tap the chat bubble on any page to send us a note directly.
- **Download My Data** — Export all your swaps and messages as a file from your profile.

### 🔧 Improvements

- **⚡ Urgent Badge** — Swaps happening within the next 48 hours are now flagged so you can act fast.
- **NEW Badge** — Swaps posted in the last 2 hours are marked so fresh listings stand out.
- **Share a Swap** — Tap the share icon on any swap to send the link to a coworker via text, WhatsApp, or any app.
- **Depot Stats** — Your depot's action page now shows how many swaps were completed this month and how many are currently open.
- **Infinite Scroll** — The swaps list now loads automatically as you scroll — no more tapping "Load More."
- **Run/Route Filter** — Filter swaps by your specific run number or route directly from the search panel.
- **Pull to Refresh** — Swipe down on the swaps list to check for new posts instantly.
- **Offline Banner** — If you lose connection, a banner lets you know instead of silently failing.
- **Vacation Week Dates** — Vacation swap selectors now show the actual date range for each week (e.g. "Week 14 · Apr 7–13") instead of just a number.
- **Clearer Swap Form Labels** — "Your Shift (the day you're giving away)" and "Wanted Shift (the day you want off)" replace the old confusing labels.
- **Smarter Empty States** — The swaps board now tells you whether there are no swaps yet vs. your filters returned no results — with a one-tap "Clear filters" button.
- **Sounds & Animations** — Subtle audio feedback when posting, saving, and completing agreements. Mute anytime with the speaker button in your depot's menu.
- **Error Toasts** — Errors now appear in red so they're clearly different from success messages.

### 🐛 Fixes

- Date input no longer overflows its border on iPhone
- Header icons on the swaps page no longer run off the right edge of the screen
- Back button from the swaps list now correctly returns to your depot menu
- Password show/hide button is now visible when a password is entered
- Login no longer crashes with a 500 error
- Past dates and times can no longer be entered or submitted when posting a swap

## Earlier Highlights

### Swap Agreements
Both operators can formally agree to a swap inside the app. Once confirmed by both sides, you get a printable PDF confirmation to bring to your supervisor.

### Messaging
Direct messaging between operators, organized by swap. Quick-reply templates, message deletion, and notification badges for unread messages.

### Push Notifications
Get notified when someone is interested in your swap, when a message arrives, or when a swap agreement is confirmed — even when the app isn't open.

### Reputation System
Every operator builds a score based on completed swaps, cancellations, and peer reviews. Scores are shown on swap cards so you know who's reliable.

### Depot Announcements
Depot reps can pin announcements to the top of the swap board — useful for coverage shortages, schedule changes, or union updates.

### "I'll Take Anything" Mode
Toggle flexible mode to let other operators know you're open to any swap. You'll appear in a strip at the top of your depot's board.

### Rep Dashboard
Union stewards and depot reps get a dedicated dashboard with swap activity charts, agreement history, and operator reliability stats.

### Multi-Language
The app launched with English and Spanish. Chinese (中文) added in the latest release.

### Admin Tools
Admins can manage users, assign roles, send broadcast messages to all operators or a specific depot, suspend accounts, and view a full audit log.
